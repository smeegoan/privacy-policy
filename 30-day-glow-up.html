Privacy Policy for 30 Days Glow Up

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

Introduction

30 Days Glow Up ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information.

Information We Collect

30 Days Glow Up stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information with external servers.

Data Storage

  • All goal completion data is stored locally on your device using Hive (local database)
  • Your start date and progress are stored locally using SharedPreferences
  • No data is sent to external servers by our app
  • No user accounts or authentication required

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information for ad personalization and measurement purposes. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device's ad settings.

Firebase Analytics (Optional)

We may use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns. This data is anonymized and aggregated. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Payment information
  • Any other personal data beyond what's necessary for ad serving

Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your app data at any time using the "Reset Progress" option in the Settings screen. Uninstalling the app also removes all local data from your device.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (via app settings or uninstalling)
  • Opt out of personalized ads (via device settings)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and within the app. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when this policy was last revised.
